## Paper Summary

Summarize the problem, contribution, and result.

## Core Intuition

Write the idea in plain engineering language.

## Method / Architecture

Describe the components and data flow.

## Mathematics

Use equations for the objective, update rule, or key derivation.

## Implementation Notes

Track code structure, dependencies, edge cases, and reproducibility details.

## Experiments

Record setup, metrics, baselines, and results.

## Observations

Capture what surprised you during implementation.

## Limitations

List assumptions, weak points, missing experiments, and failure modes.

## References

Add papers, codebases, datasets, and related notes.
