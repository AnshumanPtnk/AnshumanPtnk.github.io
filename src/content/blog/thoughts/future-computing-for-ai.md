AI progress is often narrated as a model story, but the deeper story is a computing story. Algorithms, chips, memory systems, networks, compilers, and deployment platforms all shape what becomes possible.

Future AI systems will likely be defined by co-design: models that fit hardware better, hardware that serves model workloads better, and infrastructure that makes the whole stack observable and efficient.
